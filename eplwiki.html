EPLWiki

EPLWiki is a simple mobile application designed to provide information about teams competing in the English league. The app covers club history, coaches, key players, and stadium details.

Technologies Used: Kotlin, Android Studio, Glide

Features:

  • View a list of English league teams
  • Detailed club history, coaches, and players
  • Information about stadiums and headquarters
  • Built using Kotlin and Glide for image loading
